Free Fast Shipping on Orders $150+ & Free Returns | Details
Enjoy free fast shipping on orders of $150 or more and free returns at RalphLauren.com only. The shipping offer is automatically applied at checkout when Fast shipping is selected and the threshold is reached in a single transaction. Orders arrive within 3 to 4 business days if orders are placed by 3 PM ET (11 AM ET for orders containing customized items). Orders containing fragrances, rugs, or lighting and orders greater than 30 units are not eligible for Fast shipping. Regular charges will apply to all other shipping methods. Amounts donated to the Pink Pony Fund do not count toward the threshold amount. This offer has no cash value. This offer is not applicable to purchases being shipped internationally.
