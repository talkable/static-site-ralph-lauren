Enjoy free shipping on orders made using a RalphLauren.ca account. Enjoy free returns within 30 days of the order ship date. See our return policy for details and exclusions. The free shipping offer is automatically applied at checkout after signing in to your account on RalphLauren.ca. Orders typically arrive within six business days if placed by 11 AM ET.
